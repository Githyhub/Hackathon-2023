What is abortion, and what are the risks?

  • Abortion is the process of ending a pregnancy, which involves two types of procedures: Medical abortion, which uses medicines to end it, such as pills, and procedural abortion, which involves surgery to remove the pregnancy from the uterus.
  • Physical risks of abortion involve pelvic infections, incomplete abortions, or internal bleeding.
  • Psychological risks vary depending on the woman. Some may feel relief after the procedure, while others may feel regret, guilt, sadness, emptiness, or lowered self esteem.
